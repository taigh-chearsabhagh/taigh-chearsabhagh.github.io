This project develops new art therapy and digital skills services for the islands of North Uist, Benbecula and South Uist. The program will support, enable and empower disadvantaged groups embedding new skills, engendering confidence, bringing together inter-generational groups to share skills and develop ideas.

The Digital Skills project will:
- Deliver a programme of work and classes in schools and community spaces to develop digital skills.
- Build local and international networks around digital technology, arts, heritage and the environment.
- Demonstrate the Hebrides as a viable location for remote working and developing a digital skills hub.
- Run projects in coding, interface design (digital and physical) arts and heritage.
